Walrus

Known for its great DJ’s and amazing share platters, Walrus is a great club to pay a visit to. With plenty of unique cocktails and an all-round great atmosphere, Walrus is the place to be for the R&B night out you’ve been craving. With something for everyone it’s no doubt it’s one of the best clubs in the Northern Quarter.

Sounding like your cup of tea? Check out their website for the details http://walrusmanchester.com/

Reviews

Jaye

As I personally love R&B music, Walrus for me is the number one club in the Northern Quarter. With great music and great vibes there just is nothing I dislike about this club. Cheap drinks and a good aesthetic are mostly what I look for in a club, and the fact that this has the perfect music too just tops it off. I love to try new things so when a flatmate introduced me to Walrus last year it honestly changed my life for the better. No club in the city matches up to this one in my opinion, which is why it is mainly the only club I go to now!
