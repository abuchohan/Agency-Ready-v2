The Ruby Lounge

A great place in the Northern Quarter which hosts a vast variety of different club nights. With everything from Top of the Pops to Funk and Disco nights, you’ll definitely find something you love here. With great drinks and a great atmosphere, you’re always guaranteed a great club night at The Ruby Lounge.

Floating your boat? Here’s their website for the lowdown https://www.therubylounge.com

Reviews

Court

My night at The Ruby Lounge was amazing. It’s honestly my favourite night in Manchester. The best night they do is Funkademia, with incredible soul, disco and funky beats blasting around the Northern Quarter. With always bust nights and everyone there for the same reason, there’s nothing not to love about this night. Although hidden underground, it’s actually really easy to access (not that you won’t see the queue from Piccadilly anyway). The toilet attendant isn’t the happiest person in the world but nothing can take away the love I have for this club. The good vibes and the great atmosphere create enough happiness for a lifetime.
