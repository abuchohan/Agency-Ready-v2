Soup Kitchen

Just off Stevenson Square, Soup Kitchen is a great pub for a couple of drinks after a long day. With a chilled yet friendly atmosphere you’ll love to sit back and relax with a pint whilst interacting on the long tables and at the bar. Plus, the food there is amazing if you fancy a quick bite or some pub nibbles.

Floating your boat? Here’s the website link https://soup-kitchen.co.uk/

Reviews

Jaye

Soup is definitely one of the greatest places to go for a few drinks after university. Cheap pints and a great atmosphere, overall it’s just a fab night out! Soup also serves good food too, anything from nibbles to full on meals and desserts which is great for when you’re sitting around and having a chat. Everything is fairly priced and it’s in a great location so it’s the perfect place for pre-drinks or just a couple of chilled drinks on the weekend or after class.
