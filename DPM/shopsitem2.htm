Oklahoma

A real quirky gem in the Northern Quarter is Oklahoma. Home to some of the most unique homeware, gifts and accessories in the city, this is the place to go to pick up a gift for a friend or family member. So if you’re looking for something a little special for an occasion, or if you’re just out to treat yourself, Oklahoma is certainly the place to go to pick up those unique possessions.

Ellie

One of my favourite places in the Northern Quarter, love to get stuff to decorate my room with and it’s such an amazing place to get gifts! My go-to place if I have a family member’s birthday coming up and it’s just a great place for a mooch about. Some of the stuff I’ve had to save up for but other bits are relatively cheap. One of my faves is a throw pillow I picked up in there from a few months ago, a really nice touch to my room in halls.
