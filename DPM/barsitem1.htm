The Blue Pig

This French-style bistro/bar is an amazing place to spend time with those around you. Whether it be afternoon cocktails or weekend pre-drinks, with the live DJ’s and amazing cocktails, the Blue Pig will always have a drink to suit your mood.

Love what you hear? Here’s the website link http://thebluepigmcr.co.uk/

Reviews

Ellie

This is my boyfriend’s favourite place in the Northern Quarter. We both love the French ambiance and the fact that we can have a sophisticated couple of drinks on a budget and feel like we’re somewhere really pricey. It’s great for when you want a break from all the gross student bars and unions, definitely makes a change from the sticky carpets and the seats that smell like smoke in most other pubs in town. The Blue Pig overall – ace.
