Fred Aldous

Even if you don’t have a single creative bone in your body, you’re guaranteed to find something you love here. With endless amounts of arts and crafts supplies, you’ll never be short of an arty project or inspirational idea. If you’re unsure of where to start, ask the staff or pick up one of the books and get reading before you embark on a new craft. Looking for a card? Try the ground floor where you’ll find hundreds of special and unique cards for any occasion, this is also where you’ll find the perfect gift to go with that card.

Check their website to see what they stock and for more information
http://www.fredaldous.co.uk/

Reviews

Abi

I love Fred Aldous! My number 1 place to pick up camera film and have a mooch about. It’s become the only place I even consider getting occasion cards from and I love the tote bags they sell. The top floor is all like books and gifts which is exactly where I go when I have a friend or family birthday coming up, it’s great for Christmas presents too. Other than photography I’ve only tried a bit of drawing and picked my stuff up from there, I do have lots of friends who study art and all of them love to go there for their supplies and to start a new craft, and with a 10% student discount, what’s not to love?
