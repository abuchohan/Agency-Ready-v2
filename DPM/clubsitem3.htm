Soup Kitchen

Just off Stevenson Square, Soup Kitchen is a great pub for a couple of drinks after a long day. With a chilled yet friendly atmosphere you’ll love to sit back and relax with a pint whilst interacting on the long tables and at the bar. Plus, the food there is amazing if you fancy a quick bite or some pub nibbles.

Floating your boat? Here’s the website link https://soup-kitchen.co.uk/

Reviews

Abi

The Remake Remodel night on a Monday from 11pm-3am is by far one of the best club nights I’ve experienced, I love the music they play and the small basement is a great setting for this kind of night out. It can get pretty hot down there but the small bar and the tiny setting really does fit the aesthetic of the music. It’s very up close and personal but it isn’t always totally heaving with people. I love the projections they have on the walls and the merchandise they sometimes sell is amazing! The drinks are so cheap and you’ll always find a massive student presence at these great nights out in the Soup basement.
