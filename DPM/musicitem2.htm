Night and Day

Without doubt, Night and Day is one of the best locations in the Northern Quarter for live music. They host a lot of different concerts and readings in here, so always be checking their site to keep on top of what’s coming next. Keep your eye on the events that are coming up and you’ll be sure to always find something that suits your taste. Other than being a great live music location, Night and Day is an amazing café during the day before turning into a bar at night. In true Northern Quarter style, it has an alternative yet stylish feel that reflects what the Northern Quarter is all about, making it the perfect place for pre-drinks before a night out or just a great place to grab some lunch and have a chilled out drink every now and again.

Check out the link to find out more: http://www.nightnday.org
