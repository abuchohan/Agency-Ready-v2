Home Sweet Home

The land of amazing cakes and shakes. Also the land of amazing breakfast food, salads, burgers and a whole host of other American inspired cuisine. A fun and aesthetically pleasing place to eat with amazing food and cake to die for (we recommend the carrot cake) Home Sweet Home offers a lovely atmosphere in two great locations in the city. After all, with totally Instagramable food and totally over the top cakes, what’s not to love?

For more info (and most importantly, a menu) browse their website

Reviews

Court

First time I visited here I just had a shake (which was incredible) but the second time me and a few friends on my course at uni went for lunch to talk over some project work and it was amazing! The American style food is amazing and the cakes – I can’t even describe the cakes it is just something you have to try. I ended up having the chicken and waffles, it was unreal so tasty and not as expensive as I expected it to be. I expected it to be really expensive like a high end café because of the look and feel of the place but it was great. Safe to say I had a couple of pictures of the food up on my Instagram by the time we’d finished.
