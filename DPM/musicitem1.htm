Piccadilly Records

Being one of the best record shops in the Northern Quarter, it’s no doubt that it plays a major role in Record Store Day (google it, it’s amazing). Piccadilly Records offers a wide variety of music for all different styles and tastes of music, with super helpful staff and plenty of available merchandise to go with your new vinyl you can’t put a foot wrong here. Piccadilly Records has to be one of the best locations in the Northern Quarter, whether you’re a music lover or not.

Reviews

Abi

I love visiting Piccadilly Records to expand my vinyl collection. I already have over 100 records so I love to nip in and talk to the people that work there to get their recommendations. The prices are more than fair and the collections they have instore are unreal. Whether you’re new to vinyl or looking to build on your collection, you’re bound to love my favourite record shop in Manchester.
