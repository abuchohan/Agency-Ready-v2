Northern Soul

It’s toasted bread. It’s melted cheese. It’s a match made in heaven and a heart attack waiting to happen and, IT. TASTES. SO. GOOD. Without doubt one of the best places to eat in the city let alone the Northern Quarter. If you’re feeling extra hungry why not try the mac and cheese grilled cheese (yes, you read me right) or the pulled pork grilled cheese, if not, the classic is equally as amazing and just as cheesy. With super affordable prices and such delicious food, could a student ask for more? Because after all, who doesn’t love carbs and cheese?!
