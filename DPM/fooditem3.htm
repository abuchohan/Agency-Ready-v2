Slice

Pizza, but better. Totally handmade and sold by the slice, sit down to enjoy great food, great coffee, and great company in its cosy location on Stevenson Square. Without doubt, no matter your taste, you’ll always find a slice that suits you. Completely fresh and cooked on order, you’ll never be disappointed with what slice has to offer.

To see where their story started and to check out the menu, go to their website here : http://slicepizza.co/

Reviews

Ellie

One of my favourite places to go and grab a quick bite if I’m out and about in town. The fresh pizza is great and really cheap considering the quality of the product. They warm it up for you on the spot and have a massive range of pizza to choose from which is handy given I follow a vegetarian diet. They’ve recently started doing sweet calzones with Nutella (which are amazing) and now do focaccia and Italian inspired sandwiches. There literally isn’t anything bad I can say about this place 10/10.
